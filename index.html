FAQ

Have questions? We’ve got answers.

What is hardware wallet?

A hardware wallet is a small device that keeps your cryptocurrency safe by storing your private keys offline. This makes it much harder for hackers to steal your assets. It’s simple to use, portable, and gives you peace of mind when managing your crypto.

Why should I have a hardware wallet?

The biggest advantage of a hardware crypto wallet is its enhanced security—your private keys are stored offline, keeping them safe from hackers and malware. It also offers complete independence, giving you full ownership of your keys and access to your crypto anytime.

What makes Nevcard Access different from other wallet?

Nevcard Access is a contactless card designed for convenience, compatible with NFC-enabled smartphones, and requires no charging. It boasts EAL6+ certification and offers innovative features like Passkey, Business Card, and NFT Access. If I lost my card, will I also lost access to my funds?

With Nevcard Access, you can securely create a backup card. This ensures that if one card is lost, you’ll still have access to your assets using the backup.
